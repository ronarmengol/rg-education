Privacy First

GDPR-Ready & Secure Data Protection

We prioritize the safety of student, parent, and staff data. Our system is built to meet international data protection standards, ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Secure Parent Access

Each parent has a unique, password-protected account. They can only view their own child's data (grades, attendance, fees) and cannot access information about other students or staff.

Encrypted Transmission

All data transmitted between the school and parents is protected using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS). This prevents unauthorized interception of sensitive personal information.

Strict Access Controls

Staff access is limited by role. Teachers only see their classes, while administrators have broader access. This Data Minimization principle ensures users only see what is necessary for their job.

Audit Trails & Logs

The system maintains detailed logs of critical actions (login attempts, grade changes, fee updates). This promotes accountability and helps quick detection of any unusual activity.
